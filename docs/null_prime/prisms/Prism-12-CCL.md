1. PURPOSE
The Cognitive Chemistry Layer (CCL) exists to solve a fundamental limitation of impulse-only cognition:
Pure signal-based reasoning collapses under sustained ambiguity, prolonged context, or accumulated consequence.
Biological intelligence does not operate purely on discrete decisions.
It operates within stateful chemical environments that bias perception, readiness, and interpretation before action.
CCL introduces this capability into ACE without introducing sentience, emotion, or autonomy.
2. WHAT CCL IS — AND WHAT IT IS NOT
CCL IS:
A slow-moving conditioning field
A context persistence layer
A bias modulation environment
A pressure distribution system
A state memory without narrative
CCL IS NOT:
A decision maker
An emotional simulator
A belief system
A goal engine
A replacement for Dot Matrix reasoning
CCL never decides.
CCL only shapes the terrain decisions move through.
