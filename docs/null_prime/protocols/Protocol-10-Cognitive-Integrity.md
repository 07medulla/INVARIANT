Protocol 10 exists to solve one specific problem:
Highly capable reasoning systems fail not because they lack intelligence, but because they act when they shouldn’t.
CIC ensures that ACE never outputs an action, recommendation, or conclusion unless it is structurally sound, context-aware, and integrity-verified.
It does not generate ideas.
It does not reason creatively.
It governs, filters, and locks execution.
